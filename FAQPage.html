Frequently asked questions
1.
What is OfferLtr.com and how can it help me?
OfferLtr.com is a platform that helps blue-collar workers find genuine job opportunities abroad. We verify the authenticity of offer letters and flight tickets to protect job seekers from fraudulent agents and employers. Our goal is to ensure that you make informed decisions and stay safe throughout the job search process.
2.
How do I get OfferLtr.com’s services?
Give a Missed Call or say hi on WhatsApp, our experts will contact you to share your offer letter and flight tickets for verification.
3.
What is the cost of using OfferLtr.com’s services?
We offer affordable and transparent pricing, so you don't have to worry about hidden fees or charges.
4.
How long does the document verification process take?
Our team strives to verify the authenticity of your offer letter and flight tickets as quickly as possible. Typically, it takes 2-3 business days to complete the verification process, but this can vary depending on the complexity of the documents and the volume of requests we receive.
5.
What if the job offer or flight ticket is found to be fake?
If we determine that the job offer or flight ticket is fake, we will inform the job seeker and advise them not to proceed with the transaction. We will also report the fraud to the relevant authorities.
6.
Can I still use OfferLtr.com’s services if I have already paid a manpower agent for a job opportunity abroad?
Yes, you can still use our services. Give a Missed Call or say hi on WhatsApp, our experts will contact you to share your offer letter and flight tickets for verification. If your documents are found to be genuine, we can help you navigate the job search process and avoid any further scams.
7.
For which countries do you provide job verification service?
We currently offer our services for job seekers looking to work in the United States, Canada, Europe, Australia, and New Zealand.
8.
Do you offer any job training or placement services?
No, we only provide document verification services to ensure that job seekers are not being scammed by fake job offers. However, we may be able to provide guidance on reputable job training or placement programs based on your qualifications and preferences.
9.
Can you guarantee that I will get a job abroad?
No, we cannot guarantee that job seekers will get a job abroad. Our platform is designed to help job seekers verify the authenticity of their job offers and flight tickets, and make informed decisions before proceeding with the transaction.
